Brahma Tower

Welcome to Brahma Tower. Your mission is simple, just move all the disks from the leftmost tower to the rightmost tower. Remember that only one disk may be moved at a time, and it is not possible to place a bigger disk on a smaller one.

Brahma Tower

Do you know that:
If the legend of Brahm's Tower was true, and if the priests were able to move disks at a rate of one per second, using the smallest number of moves it would take them around 585 billion years to finish,which is about 42 times the current age of the Universe.

Do you know that:
The puzzle was invented by the French mathematician Édouard Lucas in 1883. There is a story about an Indian temple in Kashi Vishwanath which contains a large room with three time-worn posts in it, surrounded by 64 golden disks.

Do you know that:
According to the legend, Brahmin priests have been moving Brahma Tower disks in accordance with the immutable rules of Brahma since that time. It was beleived that, when the last move of the puzzle is completed, the world will end.

Do you know that:
In the film Rise of the Planet of the Apes (2011), this puzzle, called in the film the "Lucas Tower", is used as a test to study the intelligence of apes.
